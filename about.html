Designing a Resilient Tomorrow

Founded by Arumugam in 80's, Sri Saastha Builders. Early on, the company concentrated on building, house expansions, and repairs, but it swiftly expanded to include a portfolio of unique residences and modest commercial projects. The business's portfolio grew to include the retail sector.Our goal was to assist the client in controlling and lowering their energy usage. We continue to uphold our dedication to that purpose by engaging in a variety of sustainable initiatives. Our engagement with clients, whether residential and commercial, is centred on our commitment to the environment, which supports and complements their own sustainability programme.
